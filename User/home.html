Liceria National Library

The repository of Liceria National Library

The Liceria National Library Theses & Dissertations Repository is a digital repository of scholarly and creative works of the students of Liceria National Library.

The theses and dissertations collection contains the title page, abstract, initial two pages of both the introduction and related literature, and the references section. All composed for the masteral and doctoral programs of Xavier University. However, not all past works have been digitized.

If you are seeking a specific thesis and do not see it here, please contact the Graduate Library at 2nd Floor of the Old Library Building.
